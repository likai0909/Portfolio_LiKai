My App Design

DIY Workshop App

My team and I designed a DIY workshop app in Figma that promotes recycling. The app enables users to create items from recycled materials, access tutorial videos, sell their creations, and earn points for rewards. Our goal was to inspire sustainable practices and contribute to the fight against climate change.
